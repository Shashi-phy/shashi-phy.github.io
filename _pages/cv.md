<section aria-labelledby="interests-heading">
  <h2 id="interests-heading">Research interests</h2>
  <ul class="cv-list">
    <li><strong>Density-functional and many-body perturbation theory</strong> — first-principles modelling of electrons, phonons, and their interactions.</li>
    <li><strong>Phonon-mediated superconductivity</strong> — beyond-Migdal vertex corrections, anharmonicity, high-pressure hydrides.</li>
    <li><strong>Topological quantum matter</strong> — transport in Weyl and Dirac semimetals, Berry-curvature effects, work functions of topological surfaces.</li>
    <li><strong>Light–matter interaction</strong> — inverse Faraday effect across 3<em>d</em>–5<em>d</em> metals, ultrafast optical control of magnetism.</li>
    <li><strong>Machine learning for materials</strong> — ML-accelerated discovery and data-driven models for electron–phonon coupling.</li>
    <li><strong>Energy materials and catalysis</strong> — 2D battery anodes/cathodes and oxide surface chemistry.</li>
  </ul>
