Teaching Assistant for hands-on exercises on Quantum ESPRESSO at the Evolution of Electronic Structure and Experimental Realization (EESTER) 2018 workshop, organized by SRMIST and IIT Madras.

## Topics Covered
- Introduction to density functional theory (DFT)
- Running self-consistent calculations in Quantum ESPRESSO
- Band structure and density of states calculations
- Structural optimization and phonon calculations
