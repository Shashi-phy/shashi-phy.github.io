Workshop Facilitator at the School on Electron-Phonon Physics, Many-Body Perturbation Theory, and Computational Workflows, held June 9-16, 2024 at the University of Texas at Austin.

## Topics Covered
- Electron-phonon interactions from first principles
- EPW code: theory and hands-on exercises
- Wannier interpolation techniques
- Superconductivity calculations using Eliashberg theory
- Transport properties from electron-phonon coupling

## Materials
This school provided hands-on training for researchers interested in computing electron-phonon properties using the EPW code and related computational tools.
